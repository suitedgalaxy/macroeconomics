The Circular Flow Model

Factor Payments- payment for the factorsof production: rent, wages, interest, and profit.

Transfer Payments- the government redistributes income (e.g. welfare, social security).

Subsidies- government payments to businesses.

Gross Domestic Product (GDP)

GDP is the dollar value of all final goods and services produced within a country in one year. Final means that GDP
only counts new goods and services.


GDP Per Capita is GDP divided by the population. It identifies on average how many products each person makes. GDP per capita is the
best measure of a nation's standard of living

The Production Possibilities Curve and The Business Cycle

The production possibilities curve shows the trade-offs in the ways an economy can allocate its resources. It graphically demonstrates scarcity
trade-offs, opportunity costs, and efficiency. Any point along the curve represents a possible combination that can be produced given a certain
productivity or efficiency. The curve is not fixed â€” an increase in efficiency or productivity will shift the curve outward and vice versa.
